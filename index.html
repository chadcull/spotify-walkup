Game-Day Flow

  1. Login to Spotify before the game and make sure this browser is the active playback device.
  2. Use Our Team for your own lineup and Opposition if the other team provides walk-up songs.
  3. Tap a batter's Play button when they are coming up. This immediately interrupts any other music.
  4. Use Fade Out when the umpire is ready, or Stop when you need silence right now.
  5. The bat icon stays on the last batter so you always know where you are in the lineup.

Our Team / Opposition

  • Set Lineup Order: turn this on, then tap players in batting order. Tap a selected player again to remove them from the order.
  • Substitute: turn this on, then tap two players to swap their positions.
  • Reset Data: paste roster JSON and load it. This clears lineup/substitute state for that team only.
  • Bench: once a lineup is set, everyone not selected appears below the bench divider.

Extra Music

  • Play a Song: starts a fresh random song from the extras playlist. It does not resume a song you previously stopped.
  • Ballpark Clips: clips play from their saved start time and automatically fade out at their saved end time.
  • Fade Out / Stop: both work on playlist songs, clips, and batter walk-up music.

Legend

🟩 Green Spotify/WiFi status means ready or connected.
🟥 Red Spotify/WiFi status means disconnected or not ready.
🟨 Yellow highlighted player means selected for substitution.

Tips

  • Keep Spotify open and active on this device before first pitch.
  • Use the sound test before the game to confirm the speaker volume.
  • For opponent songs, only load their tab if you actually have their data. Otherwise, ignore it completely.
  • If Spotify gets weird, stop playback, refresh the page, and login again. Because technology enjoys humbling us.
